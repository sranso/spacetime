I participated in the future programming workshop in October. I highly recommend it for anyone thinking about it next year. I demoed two versions of my side project, which is an end-user programming environment.

Spacetime (new version)

You can try out Spacetime here. The demo mentions most of the keyboard shortcuts, but I didn't say that "d" deletes a row and "D" deletes a column.

My original motivation for this project was to explore what programming could look like beyond editing text in a file, but I'm now thinking it would be cool to make games, interactive art, and explorable explanations. A requirement is that it must be simple enough for ordinary people to learn. I'd also like to focus on making things easy to remix in powerful ways.
