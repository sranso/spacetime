Spacetime

Spacetime is an end-user programming environment that is similar to a spreadsheet, but with a focus on two dimensional graphics.

My original motivation for this project was to explore what programming could look like beyond editing text in a file, but I'm now thinking it would be cool to make games, interactive art, and explorable explanations. A requirement is that it must be simple enough for ordinary people to learn. I'd also like to focus on making things easy to remix in powerful ways.

You can try out Spacetime here. The demo mentions most of the keyboard shortcuts (more like "only cuts"), but I didn't say that "d" deletes a row and "D" deletes a column.

Interested?

Follow the development of Spacetime in the Google group, or follow me on Twitter. See the source and submit issues or pull requests on Github.
