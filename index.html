Spacetime

Spacetime is a programming environment for interactive 2D graphics that runs in the browser. Programs are a grid of cells with time as the x dimension.

My goal is for Spacetime to be a platform for anyone to make games, interactive art, explorable explanations, and other programs. (There's a long way to go before that happens.) It's being designed to promote sharing and building off of each other's work by making programs composable and copy/paste friendly.

Try out Spacetime here. The demo mentions most of the keyboard shortcuts except that "d" deletes a row and "D" deletes a column. Another graphics command is "rotate".

Interested?

Follow the development of Spacetime on the Google group, or follow me on Twitter. See the source and submit issues or pull requests on Github.
